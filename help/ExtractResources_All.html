Extract All Files

Back to Index

Extracts all files from the archive to your computer.

A list of files and directories will be displayed in the Side Panel. At the bottom of the side panel, you can enter the directory where the files will be extracted. You then click the Extract All button to start extracting the files to your computer.

Optionally, if Game Extractor recognises any images or models in the archive, you can convert them into a different format when extracting. To do this, choose the desired formats at the bottom of the Side Panel.
